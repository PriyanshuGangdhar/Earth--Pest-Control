What Are Bedbugs?

Bedbugs are small, reddish-brown insects that feed on the blood of humans and animals while they sleep. They are usually about the size of an apple seed and are expert hiders — found in mattress seams, bed frames, headboards, cracks in walls, and even behind picture frames.
