What Are Silverfish?

Silverfish are small, silver-colored insects that thrive in dark, humid, and warm environments. Common hiding spots include basements, kitchens, bathrooms, and storerooms. While they don’t bite, they can cause severe damage by feeding on:
Books and important papers
Clothes, carpets, and linens
Wallpaper, glue, and packaging materials

Why You Need Silverfish Control

Silverfish may look harmless, but they silently destroy valuable items and can spread quickly across your property. Ignoring them can lead to:
Damaged documents and expensive clothes
Unhygienic conditions
Repeated infestations
